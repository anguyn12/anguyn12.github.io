Retail Pharmacy for the future - Associate Experience

As a lead UX Product Designer for Walmart Pharmacy's Associate-facing experience, I led the user research and workshops to help the team discovered opportunities and reach alignment to transform the retail pharmacy for the future. I also delivered 3 main end-to-end digital solutions for Pharmacist and Technicians' fulfillment, dispensary and counseling workflow.
During Covid-19 pandemic, my team quickly launched the associate's Shipping and no-touch curbside experience to keep them safe while serving our patients. The solutions resulted in the all-time high productivity rate (300%).
Our pharmacists and technicians love our patient-centered solutions and their performance have improved tremendously.
